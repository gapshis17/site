About SwiftWay Logistics

Your trusted partner in global shipping

Connecting Continents Since 2015

Founded in Frankfurt, Germany, SwiftWay Logistics has grown from a regional carrier to a global shipping provider with offices across Europe and North America. Our mission is to simplify global trade through reliable, transparent, and efficient logistics solutions.

At SwiftWay, we believe in building partnerships rather than just providing services. Our core values—reliability, innovation, and customer focus—drive every aspect of our operations. We've invested in state-of-the-art tracking systems and a modern fleet to ensure your shipments arrive on time and in perfect condition.

Cargo Requirements & Packaging

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Flammable liquids and gases
  • Perishable food items
  • Illegal substances
  • Live animals without special arrangement
  • Precious metals and stones over $10,000 value

Packaging Guidelines

  • Use double-walled cardboard boxes for fragile items
  • Provide at least 5cm cushioning on all sides
  • Secure items to prevent movement inside packages
  • Use waterproof packaging for international shipments
  • Palletize items over 50kg
  • Maximum dimensions: 250cm x 200cm x 200cm

Additional Services Pricing

  • Cargo Insurance: 1.5% of declared value (minimum $10)
  • Professional Packaging: $15-$50 based on item size
  • Door-to-Door Delivery: $45-$90 based on location
  • Expedited Shipping: +40% of base price for 50% faster delivery
  • Storage Services: $15/day per pallet space

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SwiftWay Logistics ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address
  • Shipping Information: Origin, destination, cargo details, tracking numbers
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by our payment partners)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your shipping requests
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve our services and website functionality
  • To send service-related communications
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist with shipping, payment processing, and IT services
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business partners in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and deliver targeted advertising. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be indicated by a revised date at the bottom of this page.
